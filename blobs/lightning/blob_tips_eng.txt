#1: Thunderbolt will alaways target the ball
when used. The delay is short, so watch out!

#2: Thunderbolt will grant Lightning Blob a few
seconds of boost if you hit yourself with it

#3: Thunderbolt will cause the ball to cling
to the ground after the ball is hit, allowing
you to combo with it!

#4: Thunderbolt's hitbox against foes is
smaller than the one that activates your boost

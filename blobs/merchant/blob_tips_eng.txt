#1: Merchant Blob can equip charms to buff
himself, but they break over time.

#2: Passive charms (up) provide effects like
increasing energy build rate, converting taken
damage to energy, and increasing speed.

#3: Offensive charms (right) provide effects
like gaining energy when dealing damage,
faster kicks, and stronger kicks.

#4: Focus charms (down) provide effects like
damage reduction, free kicks, and healing.

#5: Defensive charms (left) provide effects like
damaging wavedashes, gaining the reflect status,
and getting passive damage reduction and faster blocks.

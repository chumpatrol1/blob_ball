#1: Blocking Starpunch will reduce
damage taken by two and prevents the stun

#2: Starpunch's damage is increased by
boosting or by hitting the enemy in their
Danger Zone

#3: Starpunch cannot be clanked with a kick

#4: Starpunch increases your kick cooldown
upon activation, but if you hit your attack
your kick cooldown decreases. Hitting a boost kick
decreases your Starpunch cooldown

#5: Missing Starpunch applies the Overheat
effect, doubling your cooldowns for 2 seconds

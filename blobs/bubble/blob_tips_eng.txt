#1: Bubbles will float around in circles. The
direction is dependent on the player's team!

#2: While the ball is caught in a bubble, 
it gains a damaging effect! Touching the ball
will hurt you a little bit.

#3: While the ball is caught in a bubble,
many abilities will affect the bubble!

#4: Popping the bubble at any point will launch
the ball, regardless of when or how!

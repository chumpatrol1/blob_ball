#1: Wind Blob can get across the field
very quickly by chaining short hops!

#2: Gale will always push the enemy
and the ball away from your goal
for a bit

#3: Wind Blob can harass the opponent
with frequent kicks. Run and gun!

#4: The yellow area surrounding your goal
is a danger zone! If you get kicked, you will
take +1 damage! Use Gale to reset the
situation and to preserve your pool of HP!

#5: Wind Blob's boost kick knocks back
opponents! Holding in reduces the effect.

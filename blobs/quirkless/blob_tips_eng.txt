#1: Quirkless Blob has the highest average stats
in the game, encouraging good fundamentals

#2: Quirkless Blob's boost is slightly more 
expensive than other blobs

#3: Quirkless Blob's fast kicks allows him to
deal frequent damage. Use this to maintain
pressure and score points through KOs!

#4: Quirkless Blob's block recharges very
quickly, allowing you to stop the ball
frequently or to parry incoming attacks!

#5: Combining your boost with a kick will
cause your kick to do +1 damage!
